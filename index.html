About Me

Hello, I'm Michael Novajosky. I am a software developer based out of Renton, WA.

I have lived in the Seattle area most of my life so technology has always been close by. I've worked in wireless most of my life so I have had first hand experience in what software does for consumers and what role it plays in every persons lives. I have been around technology working with consumers directly helping them navigate their devices, troubleshooting problems and making recommendations. Being a part of an industry that enhances the lives of others is what drives me to learn everyday.

I like to build apps people can use. Wether it is a game or a social app, I enjoy building things that gives people a sense of community. I am a huge fan of the idea of a community that is open to everyone and is always striving to make the world more enjoyable for everyone.
